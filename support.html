Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are the system requirements for ShipSight?

Operating System: Windows 10 (64-bit) or later, Windows 11 recommended

Hardware: Intel i3 or AMD equivalent, 4 GB RAM (8 GB recommended), 500 MB free disk space

Camera: Any USB webcam, IP camera, or CCTV system with DirectShow support

Connectivity: Internet connection for activation and updates

Which camera types are compatible with ShipSight?

ShipSight supports a wide range of camera types:

  • USB webcams (HD, Full HD, 4K)
  • IP/Network cameras (RTSP, HTTP)
  • CCTV systems and security cameras
  • Mobile device cameras (via network connection)
  • Professional camcorders with USB/HDMI capture

The software automatically detects most DirectShow-compatible devices.

How do I integrate ShipSight with my ecommerce platform?

ShipSight integrates seamlessly with major platforms:

  • Amazon: Direct integration with Seller Central API
  • Flipkart: Native support for Flipkart order management
  • AJIO: Built-in AJIO marketplace integration
  • Shopify: App available in Shopify App Store
  • WooCommerce: WordPress plugin for easy setup
  • Custom platforms: REST API for custom integrations

Setup typically takes 5-10 minutes with our guided integration wizard.

What barcode formats does ShipSight support?

Our advanced barcode recognition engine supports 15+ formats:

  • UPC-A and UPC-E
  • EAN-13 and EAN-8
  • Code 39, Code 93, Code 128
  • QR codes and Data Matrix
  • PDF417 and Aztec codes
  • ITF (Interleaved 2 of 5)
  • Custom tracking number formats

Scanning accuracy is 99.9% with 0.2-second recognition time.

How does license activation work?

License activation is secure and straightforward:

  1. Purchase your license and receive the activation key via email
  2. Install ShipSight on your computer
  3. Enter your license key and email during first launch
  4. The software connects securely to our activation servers
  5. Your license is validated and the software is unlocked

Licenses are tied to hardware fingerprints for security. Contact support for license transfers.

What happens if I lose my license key?

Don't worry! We can help you recover your license:

  • Contact our support team with your original purchase email
  • Provide your name and company information used during purchase
  • We'll verify your identity and resend the license key
  • For security, we may ask for additional verification

License recovery typically takes 1-2 business days.

How do I troubleshoot camera connection issues?

Common camera issues and solutions:

  • Camera not detected: Check USB connections, try different ports
  • Poor video quality: Verify camera drivers are up to date
  • IP camera issues: Check network settings and firewall rules
  • Multiple cameras: Ensure each camera has unique identifiers
  • Frame rate issues: Adjust resolution settings in camera preferences

Use our built-in Camera Diagnostic Tool for automated troubleshooting.

Can I use ShipSight for multiple warehouse locations?

Yes! ShipSight supports multi-location setups:

  • Individual licenses: Each workstation needs its own license
  • Centralized management: Monitor all locations from a single dashboard
  • Location tagging: Videos are automatically tagged with location data
  • Cloud sync: All recordings sync to centralized cloud storage
  • Enterprise plans: Volume discounts available for 10+ licenses

Contact our sales team for multi-location deployment assistance.
